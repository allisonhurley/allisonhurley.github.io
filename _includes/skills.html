Our Mission

The FIRST Alumni Association at Kettering was created to support FIRST alumni, or people who are currently involved with FIRST, and provide them with an organization that engages students with Kettering campus culture, develops them as mentors or volunteers, and provides them with opportunities to give back to FIRST, through service to the FIRST Robotics Community Center at Kettering, to FIRST in Michigan, as well as the entire FIRST Community, worldwide. This organization is a direct benefit to students who are passionate about the FIRST Robotics program. It will help them grow their personal network, as well as their knowledge of technical subjects and business. It will help FIRST Alumni stay engaged with the program that helped shape them. This organization is a benefit to the community, as this will be a formal facilitator for teams in need of assistance to get help from Kettering students, who will have expertise that those teams need so dearly.
